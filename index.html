About

Summary

7+ years building vehicle motion control systems and software, now as Senior System Engineer for Geely's Advanced Motion functions across platforms — covering system design and architecture, model-based development, and in-vehicle function calibration.

Earlier at Geely, I owned SW requirements and roadmap for L4 Motion Control as Product Owner. Before that, at NEVS, I built HIL/MIL simulation architecture and toolchains for autonomous driving validation, including as Product Owner for Vehicle Simulation and xIL Test Environments. My Master's thesis at Volvo Cars focused on EPAS steering system refinement using driving simulators and hardware-in-loop test rigs.

Five years across Formula Student — as Vehicle Dynamics and Control Engineer at KTH, and earlier as Project Manager at Hermes Racing — shaped how I lead technical teams under tight deadlines and resource constraints.
